Underwear.js.

Underscore.js is a great library, but some prefer to extend the native prototypes with these types of methods. With Underwear.js, you can use the awesomeness of underscore from the comfort of your own objects.

Underwear.js translates all the underscore methods to native prototype extensions for those who prefer to keep all the skids in their underpants ;)
